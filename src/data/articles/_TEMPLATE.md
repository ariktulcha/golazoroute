## Metadata

## Introduction / Why Visit [City/Topic]?

Start with a **Direct Answer** in the first paragraph. Be specific about numbers, dates, and key benefits.

Example:
**Direct Answer:** Miami will host **7 World Cup 2026 matches** at Hard Rock Stadium, including a Semi-Final. Miami combines world-class soccer with pristine beaches, vibrant nightlife, Latin culture, and year-round sunshine - all with easy access to the stadium.

Continue with 2-3 more paragraphs providing context, unique selling points, and setting expectations.

### Key Facts:
- **Location:** Specific address/area
- **Capacity/Details:** Relevant numbers
- **Dates/Schedule:** When things happen
- **Weather:** What to expect
- **Cost:** Average prices
- **Transit:** How to get around

## Writing Tips:
1. **SEO**: Use target keywords naturally in headers and content
2. **Clarity**: Write for someone who knows nothing about the topic
3. **Details**: Include specific numbers, prices, times, distances
4. **Actionable**: Give clear next steps and recommendations
5. **Structure**: Use headers to break up content (## for main, ### for sub)
6. **Length**: Aim for 2000-3000 words for comprehensive guides
7. **FAQs**: Include at least 5-10 common questions
8. **Links**: Reference other relevant articles/cities naturally in text
